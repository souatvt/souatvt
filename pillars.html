The Pillars

Our primary goals focus on our three pillars:

Professional

Cultural

Social

_____________________________________

We encourage our members to go to professional events, such as career fairs. We also help each other grow professionally by critiquing resumes, building interview and presentation skills, and gaining confidence in a professional setting.

SOU sponsors International Coffee Corner (free coffee from different countries) once a month in Pamplin Atrium. We learn about other countries, their business practices and customs, and become globally aware.

We bond socially at chapter meetings, at several of the social events (Pumpkin Carving, Secret Santa) coordinated each semester, and on outings (hikes, dinner parties).
